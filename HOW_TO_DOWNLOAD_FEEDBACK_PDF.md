# How to Download Interview Feedback as PDF

## Quick Guide

### Step 1: Complete Your Interview
1. Finish your interview session
2. Navigate to the feedback page (automatically redirected or click "View Feedback")

### Step 2: Download PDF
1. On the feedback report page, click the **"Download PDF"** button in the top right
2. Your browser's print dialog will open instantly

### Step 3: Save as PDF
1. In the print dialog:
   - **Destination**: Select "Save as PDF" (or "Microsoft Print to PDF" on Windows)
   - **Layout**: Portrait (recommended)
   - **Color**: Color (to preserve charts and colors)
   - **Margins**: Default
   - **Scale**: Default (100%)
   - **Background graphics**: ‚úÖ Enabled (important for colors)

2. Click **"Save"** button
3. Choose where to save your PDF
4. Done! Your professional feedback report is saved

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Charts are not visible
**Solution**: Enable "Background graphics" in print settings

### Problem: Text is too small
**Solution**: Increase scale to 110% or 120% in print settings

### Problem: Content is cut off
**Solution**: 
- Reduce margins to "Minimum"
- Or select "Fit to page" scale option

### Problem: Colors are missing
**Solution**: 
- Select "Color" instead of "Black & White"
- Enable "Background graphics"

### Problem: Button doesn't work
**Solution**: 
- Refresh the page
- Make sure feedback has been generated
- Check browser console for errors

## Alternative Method

If the button doesn't work, you can:
1. Press **Ctrl + P** (Windows) or **Cmd + P** (Mac)
2. Follow the same steps to save as PDF
